GUIDE AND TIPS

Beginner guide

(controls, maps, game modes)

Hardpoint (Elite | 150 Points) Objective-based mode where teams fight to control rotating hardpoint zones. Rotate early and defend with teammates to gain points and win the match.

Search & Destroy (Elite | 8 Rounds) Tactical mode where players have only one life per round. Teams plant or defend bombs, so teamwork and smart positioning are important.

Frontline (Elite | 60 Points) Players respawn at their base, making constant action possible. Protect spawn areas and push carefully to control the map.

Team Deathmatch (Elite | 50 Points) Classic mode where teams compete to reach the kill limit first, ideal for practicing aim and weapon control.

Gameplay Tips: Adjust your control layout and sensitivity for comfort. Use cover during fights and avoid running in open areas.

Team Tips: Move with teammates, share objectives, and support each other instead of fighting alone.

Improvement Tips: Learn map layouts, practice aiming daily, and experiment with different weapons.
